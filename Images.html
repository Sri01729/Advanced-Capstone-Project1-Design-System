Design System

Overview

Images play a significant role in modern web design, adding visual interest and helping to convey a message or evoke an emotional response. In a design system, images are often used in combination with other design elements to create a cohesive and aesthetically pleasing user interface.

When to use

Images are a good choice when:

  • To illustrate concepts or ideas
  • To create visual interest
  • To convey information through infographics or diagrams

When not to use

Images may not be appropriate:

  • When they add no value to the content or layout
  • When they are low quality or pixelated, reducing the overall user experience
  • When they are not optimized for accessibility, such as lacking alt tags


Overall,Using Flexbox, we can easily control the placement of these elements and ensure they take up the full width of the container while maintaining a defined height.
