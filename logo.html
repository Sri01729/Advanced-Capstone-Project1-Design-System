Design System

Overview

A logo is a graphical symbol or emblem used to identify a brand, organization, or product. In a design system, a logo serves as a visual representation of the brand and helps to establish brand recognition and awareness.

When to use

A logo should be used consistently on all company communications, such as business cards, letterhead, websites, and packaging, to build brand recognition and create a cohesive visual identity. It can be used to represent the company or product in various mediums, such as advertisements, social media, and promotional materials.

When not to use

Logos should not be overused or abused in a design. Too much repetition can reduce its impact and effectiveness. It should not be modified or altered in a way that changes the original intended design and meaning. This can undermine the brand's credibility and confuse customers.


In conclusion, a logo is an important element in a design system that should be used consistently and appropriately to support the brand's image and identity.
